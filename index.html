OpenTraffic is a nonprofit organization developing real-time traffic data on a global open-source platform. We are utilizing the latest advances in open transportation technology to provide access to traffic data for cities, businesses, and individuals.

Currently, any group or individual reliant on traffic data is limited to using commercial data sets â€“ the cost and license terms of which pose barrier to entry. Adding to the difficulty, many areas of the world, particularly developing countries do not have commercial traffic data sets at all.

OpenTraffic is building a non-commercial, global traffic speed data set linked to OpenStreetMap and built entirely on open-source software. Working with stakeholders including invested fleet operators, app developers, and government entities, we are developing a powerful, anonymized data set to support real-time and analysis applications.
